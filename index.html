About Clean India

Clean India is a citizen-driven movement to end public littering through community action and technology. We believe every Indian has the power to make a difference — one photo at a time.

Our platform connects responsible citizens with local authorities to take swift action against public littering. Together, we are building a cleaner, healthier, and prouder India.
